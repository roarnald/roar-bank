## Stakeholder Management Principles

1. **Flat communication**: Always maintained approachable, flat communication regardless of hierarchy.
2. **Altitude adaptation**: Technical with engineers; business framing with PMs; high-level with CTO.
3. **Proactive transparency**: Raised concerns early (e.g., pipeline delays, HR communication gaps) rather than waiting.
4. **Evidence-based influence**: Proposals backed by data, diagrams, and referenced sources.
5. **Empathy first**: Consistently sought to understand stakeholder concerns before proposing solutions.
