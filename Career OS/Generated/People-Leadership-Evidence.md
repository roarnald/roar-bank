## Frameworks & Systems Built

### Urgency Tagging (2021)
- **Problem**: Code review tension between learning mode and urgency.
- **Solution**: Workflow innovation — `[Urgent]` MR label triggers immediate fix mode.
- **Process**: Individually interviewed affected developers; piloted system; followed up at 2 months.
- **Result**: Team felt heard; rare usage = high peace of mind effect.
- [[2021-11-urgency-tagging-workflow]]

### Buddy System (2022+)
- **Purpose**: Newcomer integration + first leadership experience for aspiring leads.
- **Design**: Senior assigned as buddy; creates soft bond; reduces load on team leads.
- **Still running**: 2025 — referenced fondly by team.

### IC Role System (2024+)
- **Purpose**: Leadership development for seniors without overwhelming them.
- **Design**: Senior = Main IC; chooses own Junior 2IC; owns planning, hosting, feedback, discussion.
- **Result**: Appraiser (Joanne): *"The plan works extremely well. I can see the results clearly, and honestly I'm amazed by the individual improvements that came out of it."*
- [[2024-01-leadership-decentralisation]]

### Anonymous Feedback System (2023+)
- Added objectivity and safety to appraisal input cycles.
- Consistent with belief: *"If there's a change that needs to be implemented, it needs to be implemented now."*
