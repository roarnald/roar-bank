# Context

As Ronald transitioned from Lead Software Engineer to Software Architect, he stepped back from being the visible team lead. His new motto: *"leader of our leaders"* — become the person who empowers and grows the leads around him, rather than leading from the front.

# Problem

The team had grown dependent on a small number of senior leads for all leadership functions — event organising, code review gating, public speaking, mentoring. This created concentration of risk and limited individual growth opportunities for promising senior developers.

# Actions

**2024 — IC Role Assignment:**
- Set an internal quota: every senior must take on at least one leadership position/task.
- Delegated routine leadership responsibilities: Frontend Sharing IC, Frontend Spotlight IC, Team Bonding Chalet IC.
- Allowed seniors to address the team directly (e.g., open Frontend Sharing events, introduce speakers) without intervening — giving space for confidence to grow.

**2025 — Structured IC Framework:**
For every frontend event:
- Assigned a **Main IC** and tasked them with choosing their own Junior dev 2IC.
- ICs were responsible for: planning, proposing changes, organising, hosting, gathering feedback, and conducting team discussions.
- Allowed ICs to grow their own leadership identity without overshadowing.

**Buddy System:**
- Maintained and evolved the Buddy System (introduced in 2022) — where seniors guide newcomers. First leadership experience for many.

**Conflict Mediation:**
- Helped mediate a significant argument between a senior and a project developer in 2024; resolved peacefully with Teck Jin (TJ).

# Impact

- Observable voice improvement in seniors: stuttering, redness, mumbling faded over time.
- Frontend Sharing independently led by Jazz and Jane in 2025, extended to external company Aquarix — without Ronald organizing it.
- Frontend Spotlight received creative upgrades from Boon Hong and Jovine (non-technical hobby sharing twist).
- Self-initiated team events emerged (Muay Thai, Pickleball, USS Halloween) — indicator of genuine team health.
- Appraiser (Joanne, 2024): *"Ronald is a true 'leader of the leaders', inspiring and nurturing future leaders by setting a powerful example."*
- Appraiser (Joanne, 2025): *"On the IC roles proposal, I really have to give Ronald very big credit... the plan works extremely well. I can see the results clearly, and honestly I'm amazed by the individual improvements that came out of it."*
