# People Leadership

## Definition
Building, growing, coaching, and retaining teams; managing morale; developing individuals; resolving conflict; creating systems and structures that sustain a healthy team culture.

## Key Demonstrations

### Growing Individuals
- Mentored junior developers on debug process, code review participation, and architectural thinking across 6+ years.
- Mentored Jacky through solo NodeJS microservice delivery — his first server-side project. Result: production deployment, Jacky presented lessons at Frontend Sharing. [[2025-01-lotto6d-nodejs-mentoring]]
- Trained UIUX team members transitioning to React Team: paired with seniors, conducted consultations, managed anxieties during the merger. [[2023-01-uiux-team-merger]]
- Coached developers on public speaking — from reading off slides to drawing analogies; observed in company-wide sharing (2023).

### Systems for Team Culture
- **Buddy System** (2022): Paired newcomers with established developers. Created a first leadership experience for senior developers.
- **Urgency Tagging** (2021): Workflow innovation to resolve code review tension — designed, piloted, followed up. [[2021-11-urgency-tagging-workflow]]
- **Frontend Sharing** (2022–2025): 13+ sessions in first year; delegated organising to seniors by 2024; expanded to external companies by 2025. [[2022-01-frontend-sharing-program]]
- **Frontend Spotlight**: Weekly anonymous Q&A to build interpersonal connections in a remote-first team.
- **Mid-Year Appraisal** and **Anonymous Internal Feedback**: Added fairness and objectivity to appraisal cycles.

### Leadership Decentralisation
- Assigned every senior an IC (In-Charge) leadership role for team events (2024–2025).
- ICs responsible for: choosing a 2IC, planning, hosting, gathering feedback, running discussions.
- Result: seniors gained confidence, events became self-sustaining, team initiated un-prompted social activities. [[2024-01-leadership-decentralisation]]

### Conflict and Morale
- Investigated and resolved code review tension through Urgency Tagging (2021).
- Managed morale during UIUX team merger — conducted individual consultations; committed to honour any dissent with a custom team structure. All approved. [[2023-01-uiux-team-merger]]
- Mediated a significant inter-developer conflict in 2024 (with TJ); resolved peacefully.
- Self-aware: acknowledged and documented a conflict-avoidance flaw (2025 appraisal) and worked on it through counseling and reflection — remarkable honesty in a performance context.

### Team Size
- Helped grow the Frontend team from a small group to **30 members** over 6 years.
