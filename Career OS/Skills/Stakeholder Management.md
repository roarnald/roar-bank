# Stakeholder Management

## Definition
Building effective working relationships with internal and external stakeholders; influencing decisions without direct authority; managing expectations across hierarchy levels; communicating technical topics at the right altitude for each audience.

## Key Demonstrations

### Influence Without Authority
- **UIUX Team Dissolution (2023)**: Presented a business case to Frontend Internal Management; secured buy-in; interviewed all affected team members; executed the merger. No formal authority over UIUX team. [[2023-01-uiux-team-merger]]
- **Coin Rewrite Decision (2021)**: Overrode PM's initial "minimal changes" instruction by presenting a compelling technical case for a full rewrite. Delivered results that validated the decision.
- **Architecture Review Process (2023)**: Proposed and implemented SA Review for new frontend projects — changed how new projects are set up company-wide.
- **Team Structure Proposals (2025)**: Raised concerns about team culture gaps with external team (GOT) directly to Head of Project; proposed Game Engine Team and NodeJS/Fullstack Team.

### Multi-Level Communication
- Works with: junior developers, senior engineers, leads, PMs (Joanne, Dominic), Head of Architecture (Kar Wai), CTO, external clients (DFNN/Manila).
- Adapts communication style: technical deep-dives with engineers, business framing with PMs and clients, high-level architecture with CTO.
- Appraiser (Dominic, 2025): *"Ronald communicates very effectively, whether with internal teams or external stakeholders. He can discuss technical details with developers as well as provide a high-level overview to other stakeholders."*

### External Stakeholder Engagement
- **DFNN (Manila)**: Business trip; presented Digital Signature and MFA proposals. Received client praise for technically sound, practical solutions.
- **Aquarix (Cross-Company)**: Key point of contact for cross-company knowledge sharing on team structure and events.
- **Google Apigee / AWS Marketplace**: Engaged external teams for API Monetisation exploration.
- **Vercel Enterprise**: Collaborated to trial Next.js hosting for DYH-VN.

### Managing Upward
- Proactively covered for Joanne (PM) during maternity leave in 2024 — handled architectural discussions and team presentations autonomously.
- Reported progress to Action Plan group (Architects + CTO) with documented Google Drive research.
- Raised HR transparency concerns professionally and constructively in 2025 appraisal.

### Operating with Incomplete Information
- Stepped into Solutions Architect role for NL with limited backend/infrastructure domain knowledge — built relationships with backend experts and DevOps engineers to fill gaps quickly.
- Appraiser (Joanne, 2025): *"Whenever there's a problem and we go to Ronald, he never leaves me with any worries."*
