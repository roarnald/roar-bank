Solution Proposals: **Ticket Strengthening with Digital Certification, *21 April 2025*** \- After the business trip to DFNN, we identified that there was a need to strengthen our ticket authenticity, to mitigate fraud and make it easier to identify bad transactions. I had proposed a solution of leveraging Digital Certification, often found to be used in cryptocurrencies, to ensure data integrity. **Data Watermarking, *4 August 2025*** \- After a security scare in July, which was later verified to not originate from us, there was a request to look into data source identification. As there was an arguably bad practice of database exporting forcefully demanded from the clients, we had to find measures to protect our team. I put forth a proposal to insert random invisible whitespaces onto the replica database, to act as an extra measure to verify in the event of a data breach. One of the recent incidents involved running bots on our application, causing a cascading failure within our microservice architecture. I put forth a two prong approach to mitigate future occurrence: **Bot Prevention, *21 September 2025*** \- As it was found that bots can be run on our application, this caused unrealistic spikes of incoming traffic, exposing our application to system outage risks. This proposal proposes the addition of bot prevention libraries within our frontend applications, and a hash comparison on our endpoints, similar to a digital certificate. **Cascading Failure Prevention, *17 September 2025*** \- Bulkhead \+ Circuit Breaker Design Patterns has been proposed to reduce the risk of a future cascading failure. After the incident, I took a deep dive to understand the true root cause of the issue. I found that the bot ran by our operations team onto long running queries hogged the shared database connection pool, preventing incoming traffic from firing their queries. This proposal describes simple steps that we could implement to align ourselves towards good design patterns, e.g. Bulkhead design where opening dedicated connection pools isolates issues and prevents site-wide outages, or, Circuit Breaker design where endpoints fail fast on slow running queries, to hit the brakes on certain endpoints before a system crash, allowing for a self recovery system. **API Monetization, *pending business for requirement*** \- To assist the business team in possible monetization revenues, I worked closely with some of the external teams like Google’s Apigee, and AWS Marketplace team to craft possible solutions for their upcoming business plans.
